Scientific Background

Fisheries productivity is determined by the quality and quantity of feed. While feed quality is influenced by the content of various nutrients, such as energy, protein, minerals, vitamins, amino acids, and fats. The balance of energy and protein is important in animal feed because it can affect the microbial fermentation process in the rumen (for mammals).
