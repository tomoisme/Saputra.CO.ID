Scientific Background

Why Coal?

The condition of agricultural land around the world is currently damage, it is caused by systematic errors in fertilization methods. Conventional fertilization mostly is focus on NPK, meanwhile the plants absorb nutrients through the soil more than these 3 elements, it is causing the soil becomes poorer in nutrients, as explained in the following illustration:

From the illustration above, there is an imbalance way how we treat the soil by only providing 3 nutrients which is NPK. This method is practices all over the world and for hundred years, it is causing soil damage. In addition, according to scientists, saying that our soil is currently losing a lot of carbon, so the soil is getting harder, therefore it is very crucial to put more carbon into the soil.

Coal is the fossilized plants, so the element or nutrients inside the coal relatively same as the plant content, but the elements has been concentrated.

Beside the nutrients, coal also content high carbon which is the source of energy for microorganisms that have an impact on increasing soil fertility.

The nutrients inside the coal since has been concentrated or inert, we must process and activate so that nutrients can be absorbed by the plant as fertilizer. That's why our coal fertilizer technology is known as nutrient activation technology. Therefore, with its complete content, coal fertilizer will improve soil quality and crop yields.

Coal Fertilizer is a coal-based fertilizer, in Indonesian market call as FUTURA, and international market call as Carbontilizer.
