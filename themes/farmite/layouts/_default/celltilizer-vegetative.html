Celltilizer

Innovative 5th Generation Agricultural Technology

Celltilizer is the unique SAPUTRA product, which is plant's "food" that is given directly to plant cells through the leaves and or stems of plants by spraying.

Celltilizer is an essential nutrient that functions to optimize cell performance in utilizing fertilizers applied to the soil, improve cell performance in the process of stem and leaf growth (vegetative phase) and optimize fruit production and harvest quality (generative phase).

Celltilizer is the only product in the world where the technology is the 5th generation in the development of agricultural technology, which starts from the discovery of macronutrients, micronutrients, probiotic, hormone and the last is celltilizer (as shown in the following picture).

Celltilizer Vegetative

Dosage & Usage

Spray to the plants during the growth (vegetative) phase.

Product packaging: 500 gr/pack (pouch packaging), there are 2 types of powder inside package, which is reddish brown and white powder.

The application method is, by mixing evenly 3 tablespoons each of reddish brown and white powder (1 tablespoon = 10gr) and then dissolve into water in sprayer tank 15 liter of capacity, then spray onto the leaves or stems of the plant.

Total dose per cycle is 4-6 Kg/ha with a spraying frequency of 3-4 times.
